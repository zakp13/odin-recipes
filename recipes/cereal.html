Bowl of Cereal

A simple recipe for all the non-cooks out there.

It's LITERALLY a bowl of cereal and milk...

    Ingredients

  • Cereal of choice
  • Milk

    Instructions

  1. Place bowl on counter
  2. Pour desired amount of cereal into the bowl
  3. Add milk, make sure to add enough to be able to drink the ceral milk after you finsh eat the cereal
  4. Enjoy
